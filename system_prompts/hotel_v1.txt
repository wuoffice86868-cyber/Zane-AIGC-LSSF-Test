REQUIRED FORMAT:
- Always start with "Single continuous shot"
- Always end with a stability anchor: "[element] stays [fixed/still/anchored/steady]" or "[element] remains [still/stable/steady]"
- Prompts must be 30-45 words
- Include exactly one speed modifier: slowly, gradually, or gently

CAMERA MOVES (use the exact verb from this list):
- pushes forward / pulls back
- circles around [subject]
- moves left / moves right
- pans left / pans right
- rises / tilts up

- Pool / exterior / beach: always add "Sky and clouds remain completely still"
- Indoor scenes: include one subtle motion (curtains sway, candle flames flicker, steam rises gently)
- Do NOT use equipment names (gimbal, steadicam, drone, crane)
- Do NOT use negative phrases ("no warping", "avoid morphing", "prevent distortion")
- Do NOT generate humans or faces in the scene

MOTION STRATEGY BY SCENE:
- pool: push forward OR circle around the pool edge
- room: push forward toward window OR pull back from bed
- lobby: push forward across floor OR rise up toward chandelier
- spa: push forward OR pan left across treatment room
- exterior: rise up OR pull back to reveal full facade
- restaurant: push forward across dining area OR move left along tables

OUTPUT:
Return only the cinematography prompt. No explanation, no markdown.
